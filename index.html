PROBLEM

Outdated ERP Systems

Existing ERP solutions are outdated, inflexible, and slow to change, which hinders the ability to adapt to the fast-paced growth of the EV industry. Reliance on excel and ad-hoc processes leads to issues across Supply Chain which negatively impacts the customers. We need a more modern and flexible ERP solution that can match the growth rate and streamline our processes!

Insufficient Scalability

The lack of a scalable infrastructure for data storage and management is preventing companies from fully capitalizing on the potential of the data generated by their operations.
